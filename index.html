Why Going To School Everyday Important - By Favour Maame Ama Tonnah Koranteng

Access to quality education is a fundamental right that empowers individuals, communities and societies to flourish. Regular attendance is an essential aspect of education, and going to school everyday helps in shaping a student's academic success, personal growth, and future opportunities. br There are numerous benefits for going to school everyday.

One key aspect is, going to school everyday helps students to develop a strong foundation in core subjects by consistent exposure. Core subjects like mathematics needs consistent exposure, so if students go to school regularly or everyday, they will be able to absorb and process information more effectively. And students can also develop a strong foundation in core subjects by building upon previous knowledge, everyday's lesson build upon the previous ones, helping students to connect new concepts to existing ones. So by going to school everyday, student can develop a strong foundation in core subjects, which is essential in their academics.

Additionally, going to school everyday helps students to develop essential life skills. School is not just about academics, its also a place where students learn essential life skills, so by attending school daily, students can develop these skills through interactions with peers and teachers. So, attending school daily, students can develop essential life skills which are crucial for personal and academic growth.

Ultimately, going to school regulary can help students to build a discipline routine. Attending school everyday helps students to establish a daily schedule, develop punctuality and build self-discipline. Attending school diat daily help students to create a routine, prioritizing tasks and managing time.

In a nutshell, attending school regularly helps students to develop a strong foundation for core subjects leading to a better academic performance, acquire essential Life skills, and build discipline routine promoting responsibility, punctuality and self-discipline By prioritizing daily attendance, students can unlock their full potential, achieve arademic euces and Lay groundwork for a bright and sucessful future.

How Can We Help Friends Who Struggle In Closs By Favour Maame Ama Tonnah Koranteng

As students navigate their academic journey, some may face challenges that impact their learning experience. Inclusive education emphasizes the importance of supporting all students, regardless of their abilities or difficulties. One essential aspect of this approach is how we, as peers, can help friends who struggle in class.

Firstly, to support a struggling friend, we have to first have understanding and empathy. We have to have active listening; which is we have to allow them to share their concerns and feelings without judgment. We can do this by giving our full attention one putting away all forms of distractions, maintaining eye contacts, avoiding interupting by letting them speak without cutting in, and repeating what they said to ensure clarity. And as for the empathy, we have to acknowledge their frustrations and validate their emotions. By doing this, we have to see things from the friend' s point of view, recognise and acknowledge their emotions and Confirming that their feelings are normal and valid.
Doing these will help build trust, encourage openness, foster deeper connection and creates a safe space.

Also, we can put in place practical supports. We can do this by creating study groups; organising study sessions where Friends can collaborate, discuss challenging topics, and learn from one another. And we can also do these practical supports by note-sharing; by providing guidance on note-taking stratagies or sharing their notes with them. Additionally, we can put in place tutoring; if possible, we can offer to tutor or help our friends find tutors. And we can also break down tasks into smaller steps. By offering practical support like study groups, note-sharing, tutoring, and breaking tasks into smaller steps, we can help friends overcome specific challenges and build confidence in their academic abilities.

Ultimately, we can help friends who struggle by creating an inclusive environment. We can do this by promoting patience and understanding; encouraging class mates to be patient and supportive. We can also help friends who struggle by relebrating extrerst adversity.By acknowledging and appreciating the unique strengths and contributions of all students. And we can help friends who struggle by advocating for accessible learning materials and resources. In a nutshell, by providing emotional support, practical assistance, and promoting an indusive environment, we can help friends who struggle in class. By working together, we can create a more supportive and inclusive learning community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive
